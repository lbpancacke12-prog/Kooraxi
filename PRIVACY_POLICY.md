# KooraXI Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KooraXI ("KooraXI", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the KooraXI mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

1. Account and profile information
- Email address
- Display name and username/handle
- Country selection
- Profile image URL (if provided)
- Authentication provider data (for example Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or email login)

2. App gameplay and social data
- Your predictions/picks and pick timestamps
- Group membership, group settings, and invite participation
- Leaderboard, streak, and score-related data
- Notification preferences and language preferences

3. Device and technical data
- Push notification token (FCM/APNs token)
- Basic app/device metadata used for security, delivery, and diagnostics
- Aggregated analytics events (when enabled through Firebase Analytics)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Provide core prediction, group, and leaderboard features
- Send push notifications you choose to receive (for reminders, results, and group activity)
- Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
- Improve app quality, reliability, and user experience

## 3. Push Notifications

If you allow notifications, KooraXI may send service and gameplay notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and Apple Push Notification service (APNs). You can disable notifications at any time in app settings or device settings.

## 4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only with service providers required to operate KooraXI, such as:

- Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Messaging, Analytics, App Check)
- Apple and Google sign-in providers (when you choose those login methods)

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy commitments.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active or as needed to provide the App.

When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize account-related data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for security, fraud prevention, legal obligations, or dispute resolution.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence through our cloud providers. We rely on provider safeguards and contractual protections where applicable.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To request any of these actions, contact us at the email below.

## 9. Children's Privacy

KooraXI is not directed to children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above and publish the revised policy.
